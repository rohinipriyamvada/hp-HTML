Welcome to Hogwarts!

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is one of the most renowned magical institutions in the wizarding world. Established over a thousand years ago, Hogwarts has stood as a beacon of magical education, shaping generations of witches and wizards. Its rich history is intertwined with the stories of its founders, the houses they created, and the many legendary events that have occurred within its walls. The school is divided into four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Founders: Godric Gryffindor founded Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff founded Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw founded Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin founded Slytherin.Each house has its own noble history and each has produced outstanding witches and wizards.
