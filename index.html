Hogwarts The School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

Welcome to Hogwarts!!

The school is divided into four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Founders: Godric Gryffindor founded Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff founded Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw founded Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin founded Slytherin.Each house has its own noble history and each has produced outstanding witches and wizards.

    Features:
  • The castle is filled with secret passages and rooms
  • The castle has 142 staircases
  • The castle is protected by spells and enchantments to keep Muggles out
  • The castle appears as an abandoned castle to Muggles
  • The castle is home to many ghosts, including Peeves the poltergeist

Information to young wizards and witches

  • The sorting hat will decide to which you belong.
  • Every year Quidditch will happen. Students from 3rd year are eligible to participate.
  • **Students are strictly prohibited from entering the forbidden forest.**
  • Any use of magic outside hogwarts is not allowed unless it is necessary.
